      Ryan J. Chuang, MD, MPhil

      Urologist

      Dr. Ryan J. Chuang is a board-certified urologist who specializes in treatment of prostate and kidney cancer. In this role, he utilizes his training in urologic oncology and minimally-invasive surgery. He is adept both in the use of robotics and focal therapy in the treatment of prostate cancer.

      In addition to his advanced training in robotic surgery, Dr. Chuang is adept in general urology care and surgeries including:

      • Kidney Stone Endoscopic Surgery and Medical Management for Prevention

      • Prostatic Outlet Techniques for the Treatment of Lower Urinary Tract Symptoms

      • Endoscopic Management of Non-Muscle Invasive Bladder Cancer

      • Evaluation of Scrotal and Male Genitalia Disorders

      Dr. Chuang lives on Long Island with his wife and enjoys traveling, cooking, and brewing specialty coffee.
