      Ryan J. Chuang, MD, MPhil

      Urologist

      Dr. Chuang obtained his medical degree from the School of Medicine at UCLA prior to completing his urology residency training at UCLA. During his residency, he performed research with Dr. Leonard Marks on focal therapies such as HIFU and cryoablation for the application of treatment of prostate cancer. After residency, Dr. Chuang completed a Robotics and Urologic Oncology Fellowship at Swedish Medical Center under the tutelage of world-renowned robotic surgery expert Dr. James Porter.

      Research and Publications

      During his undergraduate studies, in addition to working in several biomedical research laboratories, Dr. Chuang dedicated his time to oncology research at the National Institutes of Health (NIH) in Bethesda, Maryland. In medical school, he conducted patient outcome research through the Department of Surgery, and received an award to conduct research through the Howard Hughes Medical Institute (HHMI).
